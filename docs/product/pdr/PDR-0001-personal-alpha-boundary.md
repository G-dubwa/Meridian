# PDR-0001 — Personal Alpha release boundary

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 18 July 2026
- Supersedes: None

## Decision

Personal Alpha is WP-07 through WP-13 on top of the sequential Foundation. It delivers local owner access, versioned journal capture with processing classes, Outlook read connection, commands and Triage, thin tasks and dependable reminders, Today, and fundamental provenance and consent ledgers.

Alpha excludes embeddings, Pulse, predictive analytics, autonomous planning, calendar writes, goals, scheduling, confirmed-execution analytics, Knowledge Library, and v1 hardening.

## Rationale and exit

The boundary proves the diary-to-dependable-reminder loop before broader planning and analytics. Exit requires the complete audit chain, reliable Outlook read sync, private-entry non-disclosure proof, and two weeks of ordinary owner use without developer intervention.

## Reversibility

Alpha capabilities remain separately packaged and work-package committed. Scope changes require a new PDR; they do not rewrite this record.
